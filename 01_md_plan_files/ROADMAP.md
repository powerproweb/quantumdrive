## Vision

**QuantumDrive.io** exists to be the unified front door for the Sovereign Systems portfolio.

In 1 year: The definitive showcase for 15+ projects with live status feeds and press kit.
In 3 years: A credibility engine that speaks for itself — every project presented with museum-grade clarity.

The north star metric: A visitor can understand every project's purpose and status within 30 seconds of landing.

### Sovereign Enduring Commitments

- The User's interests are never compromised to grow a metric
- Every project is represented honestly — no inflated claims
- The team builds work worth being proud of
- The community is left better for the portfolio's presence

See `01_md_plan_files/SOVEREIGN.md` for the full doctrine.

## What We Are NOT Building

- ❌ A CMS or admin panel — the JSON registry is the interface
- ❌ A login system — this is a public portfolio
- ❌ Analytics or tracking — violates data minimalism principle
- ❌ A blog or content marketing engine — each project has its own presence

### Sovereign Non-Negotiables (Never on the Roadmap)

- ❌ Dark patterns or manipulative UX
- ❌ Artificial lock-in mechanisms
- ❌ Engagement-maximization at cost of wellbeing
- ❌ Opaque data practices
